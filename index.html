Hey, I'm Chris!

I'm a Software Engineering student and musician currently based in Australia. I love the idea of creating something from nothing.

My first experience with coding was an attempt to make a platformer for Mac or iOS based devices. However, it never struck me as a solid career path until reading Steve Job's biography. It really got me thinking about the potential of computers for social and economical change.

Although I have many goals, one of my dream goals is to develop software that empowers and improves the livelihoods of people living in areas less technologically inclined or where freedom has been curbed.

I have observed the turning point in human development where information is instant, communication is borderless, and data is valuable. I believe this potential should not go to waste.
